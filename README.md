# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (SQL | Power BI | Tableau)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to uncover insights related to workforce distribution, employee attrition, and job satisfaction. The goal is to support better HR decision-making through data-driven insights and interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall employee distribution
* Measure attrition and attrition rate
* Identify active employees
* Evaluate average age of employees
* Understand factors affecting employee attrition

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data cleaning, transformation, and querying
* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation and visualization
* **Tableau** – Data storytelling and advanced visual analytics

---
## 📂 Dataset

[Download Dataset](employee_data.csv)

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Employees
* Attrition
* Attrition Rate
* Active Employees
* Average Age

---

## 📈 Visualizations

* **Pie Chart** – Department-wise Attrition
* **Clustered Column Chart** – Number of Employees by Age Group
* **Matrix Table** – Job Satisfaction Ratings
* **Clustered Bar Chart** – Education Field vs Attrition
* **Donut Chart** – Attrition Rate by Gender across Age Groups

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Certain departments show higher attrition rates
* Age group plays a significant role in employee turnover
* Job satisfaction varies across roles and impacts attrition
* Education field influences employee retention trends
